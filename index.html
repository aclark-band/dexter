Dexter: Rocked the party!

Dexter was an alternative rock band from Baltimore, MD, USA; active circa 1999-2001.

Members

Dexter was:

  • Founding member Alex Clark on vocals and guitar
  • Joe Ruelos on bass
  • Mike Schwarzmann on drums

Clubs

Dexter performed at various clubs in the Baltimore area, including:

  • The 8x10
  • The Brass Monkey
