Dexter: Rocked The Party!

Dexter was an alternative rock band from Baltimore, MD, USA; active circa 1999-2001.

Members

Dexter was a power trio, consisting of:

  • Alex Clark Guitar/Vocals
  • Joe Ruelos Bass
  • Mike Schwarzmann Drums

Clubs

Dexter performed at various clubs in Baltimore, including:

  • The 8x10
  • The Brass Monkey
